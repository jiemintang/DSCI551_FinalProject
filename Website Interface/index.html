Dataset Description

The “Crime in Los Angeles” dataset is downloaded from Kaggle, transcribed from original police reports that were written in papers. It contains 1,584,315 records and 26 different features accumulated from the year of 2010 to the September of 2017. On the left is the organization map of LAPD, dividing the Los Angeles County into 4 bureaus and 21 areas. The dataset records the detailed report of cases in each area, as well as the information of the victim, along with some other important information.
